Date: c.1998 (Pac-Guy Era)

Plot:
Pea-Guy has succeeded in defeating Kan and realizes that the only way to have peace is to force it with a gun.  Using his newfound power he re-conquers Peatoria and turns the peatorian people into a nightmare army.  With his army the newly evil Pea-Guy turns his sights towards another "peaceful" planet to conquer - Spegroon!  Now Two Tail, Fred and their friends must fight to save their home planet from Pea-Guy's madness.  Can he succeed, and maybe save Pea-Guy from himself as well?  Who knows...

Gameplay:
Standard scrolling platformer (like Duke Nukem) with the player aided by special abilities such as Two Tail's jumping power.
